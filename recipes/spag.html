Spaghetti Recipe

Description

Spaghetti, with its humble origins in Italy, has traversed continents and cultures to become a beloved staple on dinner tables worldwide. Its simple yet versatile nature makes it a canvas for culinary creativity, whether adorned with a rich marinara sauce, creamy Alfredo, or a zesty pesto. The satisfying slurp as it's twirled around a fork brings a sense of comfort and nostalgia, evoking memories of family gatherings and shared meals. From quick weeknight dinners to elaborate feasts, spaghetti holds a special place in the hearts and stomachs of many, embodying the essence of hearty, wholesome comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (16 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste/li>
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Â½ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine ground beef, onion, garlic, and green pepper in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Cook and stir until meat is browned and crumbly and vegetables are tender, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain grease.
  3. Stir diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste into the pan. Season with oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer spaghetti sauce for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
